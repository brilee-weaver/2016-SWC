Making Ordered Lists Using HTML

< ol >

Sketching ordered lists with HTML helps you organize content on your webpage.

Let's get started!

    How to use the ordered list tag, < ol > :
  • Begin by entering the tag in your editor browser.
  • As you would with a paragraph tag, < p >, enter a name for your ordered list.
  • Next, indent and type the list item tag, < li >. This will be the first item on your list.
  • Remember to use a closing tag before moving on to your next list item (i.e. < li >).

    • Congrats! You've just completed your ordered list.
